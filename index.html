FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How can I borrow books from the library?To borrow books from the library, you need to be a registered member of the library. You can visit the library with your student ID card to borrow books.
What are the library hours?The library is open from Monday to Saturday from 9:00 am to 8:00 pm. It is closed on Sundays and public holidays.
Can I access e-resources from off-campus?Yes, students and faculty members can access e-resources from off-campus by logging in with their institutional credentials.
How can I request a book that is currently checked out?You can place a hold on a book that is currently checked out by filling out a book request form at the circulation desk.
Are there any fines for late returns?Yes, there are fines for late returns. The fine amount varies depending on the type of material borrowed. Please return materials on time to avoid fines.
